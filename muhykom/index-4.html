Who We Are

Muhykom Freight is a fully integrated worldwide service provider dedicated to serving the Shipping, Logistics and Marine Markets through an international network. Established in 2009 and incorporated in 2018,muhykom has today grown rapidly throughout our range of services.we vertically established our growned with trustworthy and dedicate to our services to increase smile to our clients. With Freight Forwarding as her core activity, Muhykom has diversified into multimodal transportation, Logistics, warehousing and Distribution thereby offering complete Supply chain management to her customers. Since inception Muhykom Freight is providing excellent services quality to her customers worldwide and it’s considered one of the reputed freight forwarders in Nigeria.
